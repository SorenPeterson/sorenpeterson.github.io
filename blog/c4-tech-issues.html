Technology Issues

Nov.23.2014

Everyday we are stepping into a new world. Constantly, amazing inventions are created by brilliant inventors. As our technology incorporates itself into our lives, we need to think about the data it can access. We must be careful how much we rely on technology. Privacy is a big issue right now. With the recent light shed on mass surveillance techniques used by the U.S. government, it is pertanent to remain vigilant and protect yourself.

Privacy is an issue that is causing a lot of disputes. With the rise of technology and the internet comes new questions we have to ask. We need to figure out how our personal information should be distributed.
